Our Story

Where Culinary Art Meets Skyline Views

Cálido Rooftop was born from a passion for creating unforgettable dining experiences. Perched above the bustling streets of Kano, we offer a sanctuary where guests can savor innovative fusion cuisine while enjoying panoramic city views.

Our culinary philosophy blends traditional Nigerian flavors with international techniques, creating dishes that are both familiar and excitingly new. Each plate is crafted with the finest locally-sourced ingredients and presented with artistic flair.

At Cálido, we believe that dining is about more than just food—it's about connection, celebration, and creating lasting memories against the backdrop of Kano's stunning skyline.

Our Menu

Our fusion menu celebrates the diversity of global cuisines while honoring Nigerian culinary traditions. Each dish is crafted with premium ingredients and artistic presentation.
